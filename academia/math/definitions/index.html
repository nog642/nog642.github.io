Limits

To a real number

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall c\in\mathbb{R}:\forall x:\forall L\in\mathbb{R}:\left(\lim_{x\to c}f(x)=L\iff\forall\varepsilon>0:\exists\delta>0:\forall x\in X:(0<|x-c|<\delta\implies|f(x)-L|<\varepsilon)\right) $$

To \(\infty\)

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall x:\forall L\in\mathbb{R}:\left(\lim_{x\to\infty}f(x)=\lim_{x\to\infty^-}f(x)=L\iff\forall\varepsilon>0:\exists N\in\mathbb{R}:\forall x\in X:(x>N\implies|f(x)-L|<\varepsilon)\right) $$

To \(-\infty\)

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall x:\forall L\in\mathbb{R}:\left(\lim_{x\to-\infty}f(x)=\lim_{x\to-\infty^+}f(x)=L\iff\forall\varepsilon>0:\exists N\in\mathbb{R}:\forall x\in X:(x< N\implies|f(x)-L|<\varepsilon)\right) $$

To a real number from above

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall c\in\mathbb{R}:\forall x:\forall L\in\mathbb{R}:\left(\lim_{x\to c^+}f(x)=L\iff\forall\varepsilon>0:\exists\delta>0:\forall x\in X:(0< x-c<\delta\implies|f(x)-L|<\varepsilon)\right) $$

To a real number from below

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall c\in\mathbb{R}:\forall x:\forall L\in\mathbb{R}:\left(\lim_{x\to c^-}f(x)=L\iff\forall\varepsilon>0:\exists\delta>0:\forall x\in X:(0< c-x<\delta\implies|f(x)-L|<\varepsilon)\right) $$

Derivative

$$ \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}(f(x))=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h} $$

Definite Integral

$$ \forall(f:X\to\mathbb{R}):\forall a,b\in[-\infty,\infty]:\int_a^bf(x)\,\mathrm{d}x=\begin{cases} \displaystyle\lim_{c\to a^+}\lim_{d\to b^-}\lim_{h\to\infty}\left(\frac{1}{h}\sum_{\substack{n=ch\\n\in X}}^{dh}f\left(\frac{n}{h}\right)\right) &\text{ if } a< b\\ \displaystyle-\int_b^af(x)\,\mathrm{d}x &\text{ if } a>b \end{cases} $$

Indefinite Integral

$$ \forall(f:\mathbb{R}\to\mathbb{R}):\int f(x)\,\mathrm{d}x=\int_C^xf(t)\,\mathrm{d}t $$
